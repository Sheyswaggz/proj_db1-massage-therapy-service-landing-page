Our Massage Therapy Services

Comprehensive therapeutic treatments designed to restore balance, relieve tension, and promote overall wellness

Swedish Massage

The classic massage technique using gentle, flowing strokes combined with kneading and circular movements. Perfect for relaxation and improving circulation. Ideal for first-time massage clients and those seeking overall stress relief.

  • Promotes deep relaxation
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Reduces muscle tension
  • Enhances flexibility

$80 / 60 min | $110 / 90 min

Deep Tissue Massage

Intensive therapeutic massage using firm pressure and slow strokes to reach deeper layers of muscle and fascia. Specifically designed to address chronic pain, muscle injuries, and persistent tension areas. May cause temporary soreness.

  • Relieves chronic pain
  • Breaks up scar tissue
  • Treats sports injuries
  • Improves posture

$95 / 60 min | $135 / 90 min

Hot Stone Massage

Therapeutic massage enhanced with smooth, heated basalt stones placed on key points of the body. The heat penetrates deep into muscles, allowing for a uniquely relaxing experience. Combines thermotherapy with traditional massage techniques.

  • Melts away tension
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Boosts immune system
  • Alleviates chronic pain

$110 / 75 min | $145 / 90 min

Prenatal Massage

Specialized massage therapy tailored for expecting mothers, designed to reduce pregnancy-related discomfort and promote relaxation. Uses side-lying positions and pregnancy-safe techniques. Safe during all trimesters with doctor approval.

  • Reduces back and leg pain
  • Decreases swelling
  • Improves sleep
  • Reduces anxiety

$90 / 60 min | $125 / 90 min

Aromatherapy Massage

Swedish massage techniques enhanced with therapeutic-grade essential oils selected for your specific wellness goals. Choose from lavender (relaxation), eucalyptus (respiratory), peppermint (energy), or custom blends.

  • Enhances relaxation
  • Elevates mood
  • Reduces stress hormones
  • Provides aromatherapy benefits

$105 / 60 min | $140 / 90 min

Sports Massage

Performance-focused massage therapy designed for athletes and active individuals. Combines techniques to prevent injuries, enhance flexibility, speed recovery, and improve athletic performance. Can be customized for pre-event or post-event needs.

  • Prevents injuries
  • Speeds recovery
  • Enhances flexibility
  • Improves performance

$100 / 60 min | $140 / 90 min
